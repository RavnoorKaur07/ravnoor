Fun Facts About M&M's

Origins

M&M's were first introduced in 1941 by Mars, Incorporated. The idea came from soldiers in the Spanish Civil War who ate chocolate pellets coated in sugar shells.

The Name

The name "M&M" stands for "Mars & Murrie." Forrest Mars Sr. and Bruce Murrie were the creators of this iconic candy.

Colors

The original M&M's colors were red, yellow, green, brown, orange, and violet. Today's standard mix includes red, yellow, green, blue, orange, and brown.
